Camera & Housing

Powered by the ESP32-CAM microcontroller with underwater-capable camera housed in a modular 100mm waterproof acrylic enclosure. Adjustable linear slides and clamps for easy installation anywhere.

Intelligent Detection

Three-stage AI architecture combining YOLOv8 object recognition, YoloPose detection, and LSTM neural networks for accurate drowning identification.

Proven Performance

91% overall detection accuracy with 100% true positive rate, ensuring no drowning incident goes undetected.

Agile Computing

All processing happens locally on the viewer's device. No cloud dependency, no monthly computing fees, no processing delay times.

Crowded Pool Ready

Advanced coordinate repositioning and distance normalization ensure stable recognition accuracy regardless of swimmer location or pool density.
