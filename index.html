Drowning in Pools

The Silent Killer

Drowning remains a major global safety issue, claiming an estimated 300,000 lives in 2021, which equates to about 30 deaths every hour. Children are disproportionately affected, as drowning is one of the leading causes of death for children aged 0-14.

The risk of drowning increases significantly in public and private pools without adequate supervision. In urban China, licensed pools are spaces sought out for recreational activities by families, yet they are often lacking in the safety aspect. With millions of summer visitors to Shanghai's 1,094 licensed pools, these facilities face overcrowding risks and strained lifeguarding capabilities.

Traditional drowning detection systems cost $50,000-$200,000+, require pool drainage and weeks of downtime for installation, and are simply out of reach for most public pools. The financial burden of installing complex, costly systems is prohibitive for low-budget facilities that need them most.

Meet the SwimGuardian

An ESP32-CAM based drowning detection system housed in a waterproof acrylic enclosure, designed to attach directly to pool channel drains. Starting at RMB 1,500 for hardware plus RMB 300-1,200/year for software, it's the most affordable drowning detection solution on the market.

Our Architecture

SwimGuardian combines YOLOv8 object recognition with YoloPose pose detection and LSTM neural networks. This three-stage architecture shrinks data density from millions of pixels to under 50 keypoint coordinates, enabling real-time processing on affordable edge devices.

Our coordinate repositioning and distance normalization ensure stable recognition accuracy in crowded pools. The LSTM neural network analyzes patterns over time, dramatically reducing false positives while maintaining 100% true positive rate.

All processing happens locally on the ESP32 microcontroller. No cloud dependency, no monthly computing fees, and instant response times under 500ms.

Hassle-Free Usage

Unlike wearable systems that require swimmers to wear devices, SwimGuardian works passively. Installation is simple - attaches to pool channel drains with basic mounting hardware. No pool modifications, no underwater wiring, no drainage required.

Contextual Awareness

Our Long Short-Term Memory neural network uses time-variant video feeds to understand patterns over time, dramatically reducing false positives. Advanced coordinate repositioning and distance normalization ensure stable recognition accuracy even in crowded pools.

Efficient AI Architecture

Combines YOLOv8 object recognition with YoloPose pose detection to shrink data density from millions of pixels to under 50 keypoint coordinates. This enables real-time processing on affordable edge devices like the ESP32-CAM, eliminating cloud dependency and monthly computing fees.
